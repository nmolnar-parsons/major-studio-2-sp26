# Lab 01: Warmup

## Objective

Understand the basics of Vue.js

- Different members of component definition: `data`, `methods`, `computed`
- Different template directives: `v-for`, `v-if`, `:disabled`, etc

### Questions to check your understanding

- What does the `data()` function of a vue component do ?
- What are the `methods` functions of a vue component ?
- What are the `computed` members of a vue component ?
